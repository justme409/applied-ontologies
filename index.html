Superintelligence for the job site.

Applied Ontologies is building the operating system for autonomous construction companies. We are a straight-shot lab focused on one product: safe, accountable superintelligence that can take a project from briefing to ribbon cutting.

Today our knowledge stack orchestrates every document, decision, and deliverable across two flagship verticals:

  • Project Pro — the general construction command center that models regulations, materials, crews, and schedules in a living ontology.
  • Project Engineer — the civil and infrastructure specialist that pairs design constraints with real-time site telemetry and robotics control.

The platform composes a living project graph to plan, simulate, and execute work. The roadmap is simple: scale capabilities as fast as we can keep safety in front, then graduate to fully autonomous, zero-headcount builders.
